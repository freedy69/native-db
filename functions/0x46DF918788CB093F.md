# PED::APPLY_PED_DAMAGE_PACK

## Summary
Apply a ped damge pack (defined in peddamage.xml) to the specifed ped

## Parameters
* **PED_INDEX** ped: Index - the index of the ped to apply the damage pack to
* **STRING** packName:
The name of the damage pack.
This should match a damage pack entry in build\dev\common\data\effects\peddamage.xml
* **FLOAT** preAge: The amount of time to preset the "age" of the damage to (sometime we want old blood, not rfesh wounds, etc)
* **FLOAT** alpha: _param - apply this alpha to the decorations which support alpha (usefull for say accumualting something, apply is as increasingly higher values)
